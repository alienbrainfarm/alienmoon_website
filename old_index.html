Alien Moon

Sixties sausje, seventies soul.

The Heart of the Moon

We don’t play these songs because we have to; we play them because we can’t imagine not hearing them.

Based in Amsterdam, Alien Moon is a project born out of a simple love for the 'roerige jaren'—that specific, electric energy of the 1960s and 70s. For us, it’s about the crackle of a tube amp, the way a harmony sits just right, and the stories found in those old grooves.

Take a look around, listen to a few tracks, and if you’re feeling the groove, come say hi at the next gig.

Crazy Cow

The alter-ego of Alien Moon — riding on the Harvest Moon

Who is Crazy Cow?

Crazy Cow is the alter-ego of Alien Moon, born out of a deep and restless love for the catalog of Neil Young. Where Alien Moon roams the wider galaxy of the '60s and '70s, Crazy Cow grazes on one singular field: the vast, beautiful, contradictory world of Neil Young.

From the gentle acoustic murmur of Harvest and After the Gold Rush to the feedback-soaked, distortion-heavy jams of Crazy Horse — Crazy Cow honours it all. Think candlelit cafés in Amsterdam, flannel shirts, a slightly out-of-tune harmonica, and that unmistakable ache in the vocals.

It's the same band, the same stage, the same Amsterdam soul — just wearing a different hat. A well-worn, slightly muddy, very Neil Young kind of hat.
